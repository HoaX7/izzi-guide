---
title: Fruits
description: Fruits are consumeables that give you a stat boost at the start of the battle.
---
Fruits give bonus power ups. They can consumed in Raid, Event, World Boss, Floor and Dark Zone Battles.

## How to Get
- Dark Zone

## Used In
- Floor, Dungeon, Raid, Dark Zone, World Boss Battles

## Types of Fruits
- Strawberry 🍓 gives +7% Hp.
- Pineapple 🍍 gives +5% Atk.
- Apple 🍎 gives +5% Def.
- Watermelon 🍉 gives +5% Arm.
- Banana 🍌 gives 5% Spd.
- Blueberry 🫐 gives 5% Crit chance.
- Grapes 🍇 gives 10% random stat buff or curse.

<Note>You can only consume 1 fruit per battle. And can get up to 5x boost.</Note>
