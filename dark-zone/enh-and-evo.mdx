## Enchantment
Enchant your card to increase its level. 1 Fragment = 200xp

### Usage
- command: `iz dz enh <id>`

## Evolution
Evolve your card into the next rank! Costs 10,000 Fragments per evo.

### Usage
- command: `iz dz evo <id>`
