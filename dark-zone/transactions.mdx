## give
Transfer Fragments <img src="../images/fragments.png" class="m-0 h-5 inline" /> to another Summoner. Both summoners must be at least level 3.

## cgive
Transfer card to another Summoner. Both summoners must be at least level 3.

### Usage
- command: `iz dz cgive <@user>`

## Buy
Purchase a card from Dark Zone dex. Past event cards are only available to premium users. 

### Usage
- Use `iz dz dex` to view all cards available for purchase.
- Use `iz dz dex buy <id>` to purchase a card.
