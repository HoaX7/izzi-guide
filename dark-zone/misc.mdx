## How to showcase card on dark zone? How to select card on dark zone?
Showcase a card on your Dark Zone profile.

### Usage
- command: `iz dz showcase <#ID>`.

## Info 
### Usage
- command: `iz info <#ID> -dz`.

## Nickname & Favorite
- To give your card a nickname use `iz nick <#ID> <nickname> -dz`.
- To favorite a card use `iz fav <#ID> -dz`.
