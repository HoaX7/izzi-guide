## adventure
Idle adventure. Spend 1 raid permit and send your team on an advanture for 1 hour. 
Type `iz dz adv` complete to complete your adventure and receive loot and rewards.
If you fail to complete the adventure in 24 hours, you will not receive any loot.
You cannot go on an adventure when you are in a raid.

### Usage
- command: `iz dz adv`.

## Boss Battle
Initiate a floor boss battle. Defeat the boss to receive Exp, Gold, Fragments, Gems and more.

### Usage
- command: `iz dz bt`.

## Pvp Battle
Challenge a player to a PvP battle.

### Usage
- command: `iz dz pvp <@user>`.
