## Create and Set Cards on Team
### Usage
- command: `iz dz tm set 4,5,6 1,2,3` -> Sets the cards on your team in their respective positions.

## Selecting Team
### Usage
- command: `iz dz tm select` -> Select/Ready the Dark Zone team for battles.

## Equip Items
### Usage
- command: `iz dz tm equip <itemId> <position>` -> Equip an item on a team card.
