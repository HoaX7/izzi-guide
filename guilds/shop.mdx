---
title: Guild Shop
description: Purchase unique cards such as **Monthly, World Boss, Dark Zone, Custom Cards** that are only available in the Guild Shop.
---
<Note>Guild Shop is reset every 7 days. Any card purchased from the Guild Shop is added to your Izzi Inventory.</Note>

## Usage
- command: `iz guild shop` <img src="../images/guild-shop.jpg" />

## Buying from Guild Shop
### Usage
- command: `iz guild shop buy <ID>` <img src="../images/guild-shop1.jpg" />
